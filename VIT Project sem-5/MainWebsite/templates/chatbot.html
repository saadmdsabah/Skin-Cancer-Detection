Chatbot in the Medical Field

A chatbot is a computer program designed to simulate human conversation. In the medical field, chatbots are used to:

  • Provide 24/7 support for medical queries and emergencies.
  • Assist in appointment scheduling with healthcare professionals.
  • Offer healthcare information and advice to patients.
  • Help patients monitor their health and medication regimens.
  • Streamline triage processes by identifying urgent cases.
