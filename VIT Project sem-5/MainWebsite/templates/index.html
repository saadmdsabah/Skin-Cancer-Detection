About Skin cancer

Firstly! what is Skin Cancer all about?

Skin cancer is a malignant growth that develops when skin cells are damaged by the sun's ultraviolet (UV) radiation or other factors like genetics. While most cases are treatable when detected early, some aggressive forms, like melanoma, can spread quickly to other organs, making them deadly. Timely diagnosis and treatment are crucial to improving survival rates. Protecting your skin from excessive sun exposure and regularly checking for unusual moles or changes in skin can reduce the risk.
